Step 5: Lubing your Switches

If you want to lube your switches, now is the optimal time to do so. Essentially, you need to pull apart every switch and apply lube to the lower housing, spring, stem, and upper housing.

Lubing all your switches may take a few hours, but it does create a massive improvement in how the switches feel after everything is done.

While this step is optional, we do recommend that everyone try and lube their switches.

The exception to this recommendation is to avoid lubing clicky switches. Doing so on these switches can muffle the click noise and make each switch sound uneven.

One brand of lube that we recommend using is the Krytox 205g0, which can be purchased from the Kebo Store here for roughly $7.

For your convenience, we recommend following this in-depth guide in regards to lubing your switches.
